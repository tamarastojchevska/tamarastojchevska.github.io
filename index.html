  1. Biography

My name is Tamara, and I live in a small neighborhood in Skopje. I live with my close family and my pet cat, Leo. Growing up, I was always interested in mathematics. I spent my elementary school years going to various math competitions and was constantly at the top of the list. That would not have been possible without the assistance of my grandfather, a former high school math teacher. I eventually stopped competing, but I still love and appreciate math. Throughout high school, I struggled to decide what career path I wanted, but I was very certain of what I didn't want. The only option I had, after some thinking, was to apply to some engineering faculty. After additional research on the faculties in my town, I chose the "safest" and "easiest" option. That's how I ended up being an FCSE freshman, and even if I'm still unsure about what to do with my life after this, at least I have the chance to explore now.
